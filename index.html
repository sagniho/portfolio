About Me

I live where Technology, AI, and Business meet.

My journey began at 16 with a mission to empower girls in tech through a non-profit I founded in 2016.

I’ve worn many hats – Software Engineer, Product Manager, Business Analyst – and through these roles, seen the transformative potential of integrating tech and AI in the business world.

My goal? To harness this potential to create market-disruptive solutions that deliver real business value.
