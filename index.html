About Me

I live where Technology, AI, and Business meet.

I first experienced the transformative power of technology when, at 16, I founded a non-profit with a mission to empower girls in tech.

This fascination with technology continued, and I went on to earn my Bachelor’s in Computer Science from Arizona State University, graduating summa cum laude. I was also honored to be recognized as an Outstanding Graduate – IMPACT Award Winner, a recognition for those who excel in innovation and social entrepreneurship.


I’ve worn many hats – Software Engineer, Product Manager, Business Analyst – and through these roles, seen the transformative potential of integrating tech and AI in the business world.

My goal? To leverage this potential to create market-disruptive solutions that deliver real business value.
